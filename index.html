My Roadmap to Becoming an IT Professional by Domenico

Certification Platforms

There are many platforms offering IT certifications. Some of the best include:

  • Coursera - Offers IT certifications from top universities.
  • Udemy - Provides affordable courses on IT subjects.
  • CompTIA - Vendor-neutral certifications like A+, Network+, and Security+.
  • Microsoft Learn - Focused on Microsoft certifications.

Free Learning Resources

Some of the best free learning resources that have helped me include:

  • Khan Academy - Great for fundamental knowledge.
  • W3Schools - Perfect for web development learning.
  • Harvard CS50 - A great introduction to computer science.
  • MDN Web Docs - A comprehensive guide to web technologies.

Badging

Many platforms offer digital badges as proof of knowledge. Some of the best badging systems include:

  • Credly - Used by major certification providers.
  • IBM Training - Offers badges for IBM technologies.
  • Microsoft Badges - Recognized digital credentials.

Challenges and Reflections

My journey to becoming an IT professional has been both exciting and challenging. Some parts have been easier than expected, such as learning basic programming concepts. However, more advanced topics like cybersecurity and networking have required a lot of dedication and hands-on practice. Balancing time between studying and other commitments has also been tough, but perseverance and structured learning have helped me stay on track.
